Halo Studios - Halo Campaign Evolved

Currently, I have the privilege of working at Halo Studios as a Technical Artist focused on character and vehicle integration within a hybrid Unreal Engine pipeline. My work spans rigging, physics setup, and gameplay implementation, along with developing tools and workflows to support large-scale animation retargeting and destruction systems. I collaborate closely with modeling, animation, game design, and FX teams to ensure assets function reliably and meet visual fidelity and gameplay needs.

Every day, I am learning more about Unreal Engine and improving workflows within a hybrid engine environment. Much of my work has involved identifying engine implementation requirements and determining what is needed to successfully bring assets into the game. At the start of production, during the transition to Unreal Engine, many existing pipeline workflows were lost, requiring us to perform some tasks manually. While this presented challenges, working alongside a highly experienced team allowed us to adapt quickly, develop enough essential pipeline tools, and continue production. Observing how they approached these challenges has been a valuable learning experience.

Through this experience, I have developed a stronger understanding of time management and the importance of efficient pipelines. Speed and efficiency are critical to success. I look forward to continuing to learn and sharing more in the future.
